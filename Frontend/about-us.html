Our Story

From a small startup to a global translation platform

2020

The Beginning

Founded by a team of AI researchers and language experts who saw the potential for real-time website translation. Started with a simple prototype that could translate basic web content.

2021

First API Launch

Launched our first public API with support for 10 languages. Gained our first 100 customers and processed over 1 million translations.

2022

Global Expansion

Expanded to support 25 languages and launched our WordPress plugin. Reached 1,000+ customers and processed 10 million translations.

2023

AI Breakthrough

Launched our advanced AI model with context-aware translations. Added support for 40+ languages and reached 5,000+ customers.

2024

Today & Beyond

Supporting 50+ languages with enterprise-grade reliability. Serving 10,000+ websites and processing millions of translations daily.
